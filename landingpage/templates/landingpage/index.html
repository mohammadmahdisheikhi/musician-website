This is Soroush Yaghmaie.

Soroush Yaghmai is a Tar player, composer, instrumental music teacher and researcher. He studied the art of music with great masters such as Mohammad Reza Lotfi, Dariush Talai, Atta Janguk and Rambad Sadif has learned. As a tar musician, he has played along with Hatem Asgari Farahani in the "Nistan" radio program under the title "Mohzar-e Ostad". He also participated in the Volos Festival in Greece in 2004. Soroush Yaghmai is the author of the book "Rasm-e Tar Nawazi" as well as music albums such as "Nova" (instrument and vocals on the Nova instrument with the vocals of Ali Khodayi and Soroush Yaghmai's Tar) and "From the past" (string solo in the voice of Bayat Isfahan and the Rastpanjgah instrument).
